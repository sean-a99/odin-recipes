Breakfast Sandwich

Ingredients

  • 1 english muffin
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 tbsp butter
  • salt & pepper to taste
  • 2 slices of cold cuts
  • 1 tsp chives

Steps

  1. Heat up pan to medium heat
  2. Add butter to pan
  3. Cut open english muffin and place on pan
  4. Crack open 2 eggs on pan sunny side-up and break yolk
  5. Season eggs with salt/pepper to taste
  6. Add chives on top of eggs
  7. After eggs are cooked, put cooked english muffin halves over cooked eggs
  8. Flip eggs & english muffin
  9. Place cold cuts on one side of eggs and flip english muffin halves to make a sandwich
