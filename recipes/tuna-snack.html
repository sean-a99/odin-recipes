Tuna Snack

Ingredients

  • 1 can of tuna
  • 1 tbsp greek yogurt
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 1 tsp pepper
  • 1 tsp garlic powder
  • 1 tsp onion powder
  • 1 tsp sriracha
  • 3 saltine crackers

Steps

  1. Open can of tuna and drain water
  2. Add tuna, greek yogurt, salt, pepper, garlic powder, onion powder, and sriracha to a small bowl and mix
  3. Crush saltine crackers on top of tuna mix
