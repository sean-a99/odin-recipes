Beef Pasta

Ingredients

  • 2 lbs lean ground beef
  • 10oz Rigatoni pasta
  • 1 jar tomato sauce
  • 4 tbsp all purpose flour
  • 1 tsp oregano
  • 1 tsp garlic powder
  • salt & pepper to taste
  • 1 cup beef broth

Steps

  1. Boil 4 quarts of water and cook pasta in water for 7 minutes
  2. Brown ground beef in large skillet
  3. Combine flour, oregano, garlic powder, salt, and pepper in small mixing bowl (dry mix)
  4. Combine tomato sauce and beef broth in large mixing bowl (wet mix)
  5. Add dry mix to browned beef and stir for one minute
  6. Add wet mix to browned beef and let simmer for 10-15 minutes
  7. Combine cooked pasta with beef mixture and serve
